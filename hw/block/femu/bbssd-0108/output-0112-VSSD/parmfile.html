*Example 3: Two runs, two raw disks, two workloads.

sd=sd1,lun=/dev/nvme0n1p1,openflags=o_direct,threads=16,size=1.5g
sd=sd2,lun=/dev/nvme0n1p2,openflags=o_direct,threads=16,size=0.5g
sd=sd3,lun=/dev/nvme0n1p3,openflags=o_direct,threads=16,size=1.5g
sd=sd4,lun=/dev/nvme0n1p4,openflags=o_direct,threads=16,size=5g
wd=wd1,sd=sd1,xfersize=4096,seekpct=10,rdpct=0
wd=wd2,sd=sd2,xfersize=4096,seekpct=50,rdpct=20
wd=wd3,sd=sd3,xfersize=4096,seekpct=100,rdpct=0,range=(1,50)
wd=wd4,sd=sd4,xfersize=4096,seekpct=100,rdpct=100
rd=run1,wd=wd*,iorate=max,elapsed=600,interval=15,warmup=0

*Run1: Two raw disks with a combined workload of 50 4k i/o's per second, r/w ratio of 3:1, and a workload of 50 8k reads per second. Run2: same with twice the i/o rate.

*This can also be run as:
*rd=run1,wd=(wd1,wd2),iorate=(100,200),elapsed=10,interval=1
